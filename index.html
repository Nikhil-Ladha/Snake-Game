Welcome to the Classic Snake Game

How To Play

The game is very simple. All you have to do is control the Snake, and move it towards the Rat () to eat it. Once, the head of the Snake touches/eat's the Rat (), the size of the Snake will increase by 1 square box and your score will increment by 1. If the snake touches the wall/block (read Instructions before the level start) or if the head of Snake touches it's own body, then...

GAME OVER!
